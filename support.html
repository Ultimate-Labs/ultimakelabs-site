How to install Applydin

1
Add to Chrome

Visit the Applydin page on the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome". Confirm the permissions when prompted.

2
Go to LinkedIn

Open linkedin.com/jobs and search for any role. The Applydin panel will be available via the blue button on the right edge of the page.

3
Open the panel

Click the blue Applydin icon on the right edge of LinkedIn — or click the Applydin icon in your Chrome toolbar — to open the side panel.

4
Save your first job

Hover over any job card and click the bookmark icon that appears. Your job is instantly saved to the Tracker tab.

Report a bug

Found something broken? We fix bugs fast. Before reporting, please check:

  • You're on a linkedin.com page (not another site)
  • You've refreshed the LinkedIn page after installing
  • The extension is enabled in chrome://extensions
  • You're using the latest version of Chrome

If the issue persists, email us with as much detail as possible — what you were doing, what happened, and which LinkedIn page you were on.
