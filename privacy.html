Privacy Policy

Filterdin Browser Extension Effective: March 2026 Version 0.9 (Beta)
TL;DR

Filterdin collects nothing. All your data stays on your device. We make zero network requests. No tracking, no analytics, no servers.

Overview

Filterdin is a Chrome browser extension built by Ultimake Labs. It adds advanced job filtering, bookmarking, and application tracking features to LinkedIn's job search feed.

This policy explains what data Filterdin does and does not collect, and how your information is handled.

Data We Collect

Filterdin does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect your LinkedIn activity, job searches, or browsing history
  • We do not collect your filter settings, saved jobs, or tracker data
  • We do not use cookies, pixels, or any tracking technologies
  • We do not run analytics or crash reporting of any kind
Local Storage

All data generated by Filterdin — including your filter preferences, bookmarked jobs, application tracker entries, and resume keywords — is stored exclusively in your browser using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API.

This means your data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server, including ours
  • Is only accessible by you, on your machine
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the extension
Network Requests

Filterdin makes zero external network requests. The extension operates entirely within your browser on the LinkedIn jobs page. It reads and modifies the page's existing content — it does not fetch data from, or send data to, any external service or API.

Third Parties

Filterdin does not share any data with third parties because it does not collect any data to share. No advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers are involved in any way.

LinkedIn

Filterdin operates on LinkedIn's website but is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to LinkedIn Corporation in any way. Filterdin only reads content that LinkedIn already renders in your browser — it does not access your LinkedIn account, credentials, or private data.

Children's Privacy

Filterdin is intended for use by job seekers and professionals. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Filterdin after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
